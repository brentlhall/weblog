Who, What, When, Where
======================
May 21, 2026

All that is needed to share news is the who, what, when and where.
*
If the news you're sharing includes why in any way, you're sharing your opinion not news.
*
In the past, news in all forms of media, was simply the who, what, when, and where of events. The why was always reserved for a chief editor in an editorial.
*
Now, the news fills a much larger time bracket and includes more opinion than news.
*
This is news that should be avoided. If you must have news, look for a source of simple short news.
*
By doing so, you can form your on opinions and you'll not be misled by another's opinion.
